Herbal Clay Mask

$24.99

A purifying mask crafted with a blend of natural clays and botanical extracts. It gently draws out impurities, detoxifies pores, and leaves your skin feeling fresh and revitalized.

Ingredients

  • Kaolin Clay, Bentonite Clay, Green Tea Extract, Chamomile Powder, Lavender Essential Oil.

How to Use

Mix a small amount with water to form a paste. Apply to clean face, avoiding the eye area. Let it sit for 10-15 minutes, then rinse thoroughly with warm water.

Pros & Cons

Pros: Deep cleansing, soothes skin, all-natural ingredients. Cons: May be drying for very sensitive skin, requires mixing before use.

Warnings

For external use only. Discontinue use if irritation occurs. Patch test on a small area of skin before full application.
