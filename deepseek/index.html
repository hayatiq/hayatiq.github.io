Our Story

Handmade with love

Founded in 2020, Hayatiq began as a small kitchen experiment and grew into a passion for creating clean, effective beauty products. Each item is carefully crafted by hand in small batches to ensure quality and freshness.

We believe in transparency, sustainability, and the power of nature to nurture your skin. Our products are free from harsh chemicals, parabens, and synthetic fragrances.
